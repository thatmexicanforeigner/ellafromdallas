What brought me here?

Who doesn't like milk tea? Or living in a place you can afford to work less than 10 hours a week? To make this island more attractive, the Taiwanese people are always eager to welcome foreigners. To be honest, I didn't know any of this before moving there. So what on earth posessed me to move to the other side of the globe without having visted first?

I had been learning Chinese Mandarin for two years in the States and I thought I had the basic phrases down. Could I hold a conversation down? I mean, define "hold". All jokes aside, I really thought my Mandarin was okay. Boy, was I wrong. In a way, this miscalculation on my end was alright. I was hoping to learn quicker through the inmersion method. I planned on living in Taiwan for a maximum of 6 months. A year and a half later, though, I was on to the next adventure in tears. I was going to miss this small island.
